About the Hackathon

Join us at the Cary Library for an exciting 9-hour, in-person hackathon! Collaborate with fellow tech enthusiasts to create innovative projects and compete for amazing prizes.

FAQs

  • What is a hackathon? A hackathon is an event where people come together to create tech projects within a limited time.
  • Who can participate? Anyone with an interest in technology, regardless of experience.
  • Do I need coding experience? No, we welcome participants of all skill levels.
  • Do I need a team? No, you can join as an individual or form a team at the event.
  • Will food be provided? Yes, meals and snacks will be provided throughout the event.
  • What should I bring? Bring your laptop, charger, and any other tools you need for coding.
  • Where will it take place? The hackathon will be held at the Cary Library, 315 Kildaire Farm Rd, Cary, NC 27511.
  • How much does it cost? The event is completely free!
